Purpose of the Application

The primary purpose of the MedJourney mobile application is to provide a secure, inter-institutional networking and mentorship platform for the medical academic community.

The Application enables verified medical students and professors to discover and connect with peers and mentors through a consent-based discovery interface. Users utilize the platform to facilitate professional networking, academic collaboration, and informal mentorship based on shared educational interests, institutional affiliations, and career goals.

Key functionalities include:

Profile management, real-time messaging, the formation of academic interest groups, and advanced search filtering to support peer-to-peer and faculty-student professional development.
